moinworld Developer Meetups

Meetups, Talks & Workshops & Networking Events

We also organize lectures, workshops or similar events on various topics for various target groups. We want to enable you to network with the development community in your city, currently in Hamburg and Munich. In addition, we want to ensure that all our members are always up to date and always learn about new techniques, methods or tools at the very beginning. These events should on the one hand connect you among each other, but also offer you the opportunity to get to know companies and thus potential employers, or to draw their attention to you. Meetup.com is the online platform we use for this purpose.
