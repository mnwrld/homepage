About Anja

Anja Blaß is a freelance dancer and dance teacher in the Hamburg area. She completed her three-year training at the Erika-Klütz School for Theatre Dance and Dance Pedagogy in Hamburg in 2015. She teaches ballet, Pilates, Modern Dance, Improvisation and Contemporary at the Tanzfabrik and the TSG Ballet School. Her projects include the architect, musician and dancer cooperation heroe_s as well as green_sleeves of HafenCity University and numerous projects of the dance orchestra Suse Tietjen. She is co-founder and dancer of the artist collective THE CURRENT DANCE COLLECTIVE and participated in the productions TRANS//FORM, Der Strom, KÖRPER//RAUM, Der Flug and KÖRPER//ICH. The video performance TRANS//FORM, created in cooperation with 747 studios GmbH, won first prize for young artists at the Virtual Reality Arts Festival (VRAHM) 2018.

Anja’s Talk

Getting up, swinging, switching off at moinblockchain there will be a short session with Anja, where everyone can relax and switch off for a moment with light standing exercises. The whole body will move from the tips of the toes to the apex, training coordination and strengthening and flexibility of the muscles for a healthier and more conscious body feeling - and hopefully a smile on the lips.
